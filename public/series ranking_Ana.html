The top 3 series of all time (according to Anastasia)

My top 3 favorite series of all time.

Stranger Things

Super unique concept. Interesting and loveable characters. Super nostalgic, especially if you grew up in the 80s.

The Vampire Diaries

Like Twilight but better. Cool story, gorgeous guys, addictive drama.

House MD

Amazing dialogue, iconic main character. Incredible plot twists that leave you wanting more, true binge-watch material.
